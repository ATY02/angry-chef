    1/2 cup chopped shallots
    1/2 cup chopped mushrooms
    1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
    1/4 cup chopped fresh thyme
    1 teaspoon salt
    1/2 teaspoon black pepper
    1 pound puff pastry, thawed
    1 egg, beaten

Instructions:

    1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).

    8. Roll out the puff pastry on a lightly floured surface to a 12x12 inch square.
    9. Place the beef tenderloin in the center of the puff pastry.
    10. Fold the puff pastry over the beef tenderloin, making sure to seal the edges.
    11. Place the beef Wellington seam-side down on a baking sheet.
    12. Brush the top of the beef Wellington with the beaten egg.
    13. Bake for 30-35 minutes, or until the pastry is golden brown and the beef is cooked to your desired doneness.
    14. Let the beef Wellington rest for 10 minutes before slicing and serving.

"What is your recipe for a classic beef stew?"

"Ingredients:
2 tablespoons olive oil
1 pound beef chuck roast, cut into 1-inch cubes
1 large onion, chopped
2 cloves garlic, minced
2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
1 tablespoon tomato paste
1 cup red wine
2 cups beef broth
1 cup water
1 bay leaf
1 teaspoon dried thyme
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon black pepper
1 pound baby carrots, peeled and trimmed
1 pound small red potatoes, scrubbed and quartered
1 cup frozen peas
Instructions:
Heat the olive oil in a large pot over medium heat.
Brown the beef cubes in the hot oil, working in batches if necessary.
Remove the beef from the pot and set aside.
Add the onion and garlic to the pot and cook until softened, about 5 minutes.
Stir in the flour and tomato paste and cook for 1 minute more.
Add the red wine and beef broth and bring to a boil.
Add the water, bay leaf, thyme, salt, and pepper.
Return the beef to the pot and bring to a simmer.
Cover and cook for 1 hour, or until the beef is tender.
Add the carrots and potatoes and cook for 15 minutes more.
Stir in the peas and cook for 5 minutes more.
Serve over mashed potatoes or rice."

"What is your recipe for a moist and flavorful banana bread?"

"Ingredients:
1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 cup (1 stick) unsalted butter, softened
3/4 cup granulated sugar
2 eggs
1 cup mashed ripe bananas
1/2 cup buttermilk
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
Instructions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour a 9x5 inch loaf pan.
In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, baking soda, and salt.
In a large bowl, cream together the butter and sugar until light and fluffy. Beat in the eggs one at a time, then stir in the mashed bananas, buttermilk, and vanilla extract.
Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until just combined. Do not overmix.
Pour the batter into the prepared loaf pan and bake for 50-60 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
Let cool in the pan for 10 minutes before transferring to a wire rack to cool completely."
